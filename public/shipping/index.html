Shipping & Policies

We offers FREE shipping on ALL orders globally, no minimum purchase. Destinations we regularly ship to include; Australia, USA, Canada, the United Kingdom, Mexico, South Africa, India, Indonesia, Japan, Germany, France, Spain, Sweden, Ireland and the rest of the EU. If your country is not on this list, not to fear we can still send get our items to you.

Many of our items are sourced in bulk from global suppliers, so our products usually take between 10-30 days to arrive, though in extremely rare circumstances can take longer. Delivery on all items is included in the price. Don't be alarmed if you don't receive all items in a large order at once, we don't like to keep our dear customers waiting so we send each item as and when it arrives.

Shipping is via local postal services once dispatched from the origin, and hence shipping times can vary seasonally.

We offer free shipping to: United Kingdom, Ireland, Canada, USA, Mexico, Europe, Australia, New Zealand, India, South Africa, South America. If you would like to find out about additional countries, please check with us first.

We advise that delivery times to Ireland may be impacted by customs procedures, lengthening the time taken to receive the product.
